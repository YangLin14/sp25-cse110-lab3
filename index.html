New Business

New Characters

Castorice (Remembrance • Quantum) - 5-star character who summons the "Death Dragon" spirit to fight alongside her. The Death Dragon deals more damage by consuming its own HP and can take damage for endangered allies.

Anaxa (Erudition • Wind) - 5-star character who can quickly apply multiple weaknesses to enemy groups. Attacking enemies with many weaknesses triggers additional free skill attacks. Ultimate skill can apply 7 types of weaknesses to all enemies.
